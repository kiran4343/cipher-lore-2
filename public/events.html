What to Expect at an IPS Investigation

🎓

Pre-Investigation Briefing

Every event begins with a thorough briefing covering the site's history, the equipment we'll use, and the protocols we follow. Safety briefing included.

🔦

Live Investigation

Participants join experienced IPS investigators for real-time paranormal investigation using professional equipment. All equipment is provided.

📊

Evidence Review

Immediately after the investigation, the team reviews evidence together. Participants learn to analyse EVP recordings, thermal footage and EMF data.

Organise a Private Investigation

IPS accepts private investigation requests for homes, businesses and historically significant locations. Get in touch to discuss how we can help.
