About the Paranormal Cipher

India's most dedicated organization for the scientific investigation of paranormal phenomena — operating with integrity, curiosity and rigorous methodology.

Home›About
Our Mission

Investigating the Unexplained with Science & Integrity

The Paranormal Cipher was founded on a simple but profound principle: paranormal phenomena deserve serious, scientific investigation — neither dismissed out of hand nor uncritically accepted.

We believe that rigorous investigation, honest documentation, and open-minded analysis are the only paths to genuine understanding of the unexplained. Every Paranormal Cipher investigation is conducted with the same care and precision as any scientific study.

We neither set out to prove nor disprove paranormal activity. We investigate, document, and present our findings — letting the evidence speak for itself.

Our Core Values

🔬
Scientific Rigor

🤝
Integrity

Our Story

A History of Discovery

2008

Paranormal Cipher is Founded

Dr. Arvind Sharma and Priya Nair establish the Paranormal Cipher in New Delhi with a team of 6 investigators. The first investigation: a reported haunting in a colonial-era mansion in Old Delhi.

2011

The Bhangarh Investigation

Paranormal Cipher conducts its first major investigation at Bhangarh Fort. The findings — including Class A EVP recordings — bring national attention to the society and establish its reputation for serious research.

2014

First Annual Conference

Paranormal Cipher hosts its first Annual Paranormal Research Conference in New Delhi, drawing over 200 attendees from 12 states. The conference establishes a national community of paranormal researchers.

2018

Research Lab Established

Paranormal Cipher opens a dedicated research laboratory for audio analysis, equipment development and evidence review. The lab's custom EVP analysis software becomes a standard tool in the field.

Today

Nationwide Presence

With over 120 active investigators across 28 states, Paranormal Cipher continues to lead paranormal research in India. Our database contains over 1,200 documented cases, 5,000+ EVP recordings, and countless hours of investigation footage.

Process

Our Investigation Methodology

Every Paranormal Cipher investigation follows a rigorous, multi-phase protocol designed to maximize evidence quality and minimize false positives.

1️⃣

Pre-Investigation Research

Historical research, site survey, witness interviews, baseline environmental measurements (EMF, temperature, humidity, barometric pressure) and equipment calibration.

2️⃣

Active Investigation

Systematic site sweep with multi-sensor arrays, simultaneous EVP sessions at multiple locations, trigger object placement, continuous video and audio recording, real-time data logging.

3️⃣

Evidence Analysis

All recordings reviewed independently by at least three team members. EVP subjected to spectral analysis. Thermal anomalies cross-referenced with environmental data. Findings compiled in formal reports.

4️⃣

Peer Review

Significant findings submitted to peer review by external investigators. Natural explanations are methodically eliminated before paranormal hypotheses are considered.

5️⃣

Report & Archive

All investigation results published in our case database regardless of findings. Negative results are as valuable as positive — they establish what is not paranormal.

6️⃣

Follow-Up

Compelling cases receive follow-up investigations 6-12 months later to test reproducibility. Clients receive full reports and are supported throughout the process.
