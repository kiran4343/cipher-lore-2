About Paranormal Cipher

India's Most Dedicated Paranormal Research Team

The Paranormal Cipher (PCL) is a dedicated organization of researchers, scientists, and investigators committed to exploring and documenting paranormal phenomena across India.

Founded in 2008 by Dr. Arvind Sharma and Priya Nair, Paranormal Cipher has grown from a two-person team into a nationwide organization with over 120 members. We approach every investigation with scientific rigor and an open mind.

Services

What We Investigate

Using cutting-edge technology alongside traditional methods to explore the full spectrum of paranormal phenomena.

👻

Ghost Investigations

Full-scale investigations of reportedly haunted locations using EVP, EMF, thermal imaging and spirit communication techniques.

📡

EVP Analysis

Professional Electronic Voice Phenomena recording and analysis using calibrated digital recorders and proprietary software.

🌡️

Thermal Imaging

FLIR thermal cameras to detect anomalous temperature variations that may indicate paranormal energy presence.

⚡

EMF Detection

High-sensitivity electromagnetic field meters to detect unexplained energy fluctuations at investigation sites.

📸

Spirit Photography

Full-spectrum and infrared photography capturing light beyond the visible spectrum for potential paranormal evidence.

📚

Historical Research

Deep historical investigation of sites, cross-referencing local legends, records and first-hand accounts.
