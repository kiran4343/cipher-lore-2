FAQ

Common Questions

Do you charge for investigations?

Private residential investigations are conducted free of charge as part of our mission. Commercial properties and events may have associated costs depending on scope.

How do I report a paranormal experience?

Use the contact form above or email us directly. Please describe the experience in as much detail as possible — date, time, location, what you saw/heard/felt, and any witnesses.

Can I join Paranormal Cipher as an investigator?

Yes! We regularly recruit new investigators. No prior experience is necessary, but a commitment to scientific methodology and ethical conduct is essential. Contact us expressing your interest.

How long does an investigation take?

Most investigations span one to three nights. Historical research and preparation begin weeks in advance, and post-investigation analysis can take several weeks for complex cases.
